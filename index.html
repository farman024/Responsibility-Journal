The Hero's Code

// RESPONSIBILITY JOURNAL · WEB OF IMPACT

I do not wear a mask to be a hero. My identity is built in silence — in the trades I make with discipline, the people I help without announcement, and the weight I carry without complaint. I am not validated by who sees me, nor broken by who doesn't. Like Peter Parker, I understand that the suit was never the source of strength — it was always the person underneath. Every responsibility I carry is a web I chose to cast, connecting me to the world not by power, but by purpose. When I am tired, I remember: the wall-crawler did not stop because the city was heavy. He stopped only when the work was done. I am the work. I am the heart. I carry on.

— FARMAN J  ·  FALCON'S HUNT
